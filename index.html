About Me

Hello world! I'm Amaya Andrews, a designer, developer, and full-time nerd. My background was originally mathematics and astrophysics, but I have long been a hobby artist and aesthetics enthusiast. I had dabbled in web development in my free time, as well as in a professional role as a quantum software engineer. There I worked in a team to write a quantum simulator back-end, as well as a front-end user manual and an introduction to quantum computing.

As the 2019 pandemic changed the job market, I became uanble to return to this job due to border closures. I decided to turn that unfortunate situation into an opportunity to pursue a life-long dream -- attending art school! I began working on a degree in Illustration, but I missed the joy of coding. Having a strong foundation in programming from my STEM experience, I taught myself more complete HTML, CSS, and JavaScript and am always sharpening my tech and design skills!
